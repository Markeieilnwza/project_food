About FoodShare

Smarter recipe planning for everyday cooking

Nowadays, more people are cooking their own meals for various reasons, such as saving money, controlling nutrition, and taking better care of their health. However, finding recipes that truly match users’ needs can still be difficult. Most recipes cannot adjust ingredient quantities based on the number of servings, lack clear nutritional information, and do not support searches based on specific conditions, such as available ingredients, preparation time, or special dietary requirements.

In addition, when users need to prepare food for several people or host guests, they often spend a significant amount of time planning the menu. This can make meal preparation more complicated and less convenient.

For these reasons, our team proposes the development of an intelligent recipe search and management system. This website will collect easy-to-follow recipes or dishes that can be prepared in advance, while also supporting automatic ingredient adjustment based on the number of servings. The goal is to reduce the complexity of meal planning and make food preparation more convenient for users.

Features

What users can do

  • Browse recipes with filters for time, difficulty, budget, and dietary tags.
  • Create and submit recipes for review.
  • Save favorites, rate dishes, and join discussions with comments.
